Transit Course! is my Android solo-developed Unity game. I designed, programmed, and created all assets. It's an endless runner with procedural levels, smooth controls, and optimized performance. The game features custom 2D and 3D art, a score-based progression system, and an emphasis on replayability. Developed using Unity and C#. It was published on Google Play.
